About Us

Located right on the beachfront in Hibberdene, The Jolly Roger Fun Pub & Grub is a relaxed coastal restaurant where locals and holidaymakers gather for great food and ocean views. Enjoy burgers, pizza, seafood, and refreshing drinks while the kids enjoy putt-putt and waterslides.

Captain's Favourites

⭐ English Style Fish & Chips

Golden battered hake served with crispy chips and tartar sauce. A true seaside classic and one of the Jolly Roger’s most loved dishes.

Pirate Burger

Juicy 200g steak burger stacked with flavour and served with crispy chips.

Chicken Schnitzel & Chips

Crumbed chicken schnitzel fried to perfection and served with golden chips.

Seafood De Lux Pasta

A rich and creamy tomato pasta loaded with delicious mixed seafood.

Chicken Mayo Pizza

A crowd favourite topped with chicken, creamy mayonnaise and mozzarella.

Double Cheese Burger

Two juicy beef patties layered with melted cheese for serious burger lovers.

Greek Salad

Crisp lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, olives and feta with a fresh dressing.
